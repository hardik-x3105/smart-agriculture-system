Our Vision for Modern Farming

Bridging the gap between agricultural tradition and Artificial Intelligence.

Project Origins

Smart Harvest originated as a specialized Smart Agriculture Decision Support System developed for the Smart India Hackathon (SIH). Initially conceptualized to solve critical agricultural challenges for the Government of Jharkhand, the system has evolved into a comprehensive platform serving farmers across Gujarat and other regions.

Core Objectives
Maximize Yield

Utilizing historical data to suggest the most productive crops based on soil composition.

Soil Preservation

Providing precise fertilizer recommendations to prevent chemical misuse and protect soil health.

Informed Decisions

Enabling farmers to forecast production volume before the season starts using predictive modeling.

Intelligence Stack

Our platform leverages a sophisticated suite of Machine Learning models to provide real-time decision support:

Crop Recommendation Model Fertilizer Advisory Model Yield Prediction Estimator Random Forest Algorithm Soil NPK Analysis Climate Data Integration

Empowering the Backbone of Our Nation

Agriculture is the primary livelihood for a vast majority of the population in regions like Jharkhand and Gujarat. By providing accessible, AI-driven insights, we aim to transform farming from a labor-intensive gamble into a data-driven science. Our platform is designed to be intuitive, ensuring that technology serves the farmer, rather than complicating their work.
